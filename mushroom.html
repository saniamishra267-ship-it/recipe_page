Mushroom Chilli

⬅ Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 250 g button mushrooms, cleaned & quartered
  • 4 tbsp cornflour (cornstarch)
  • 3 tbsp maida (all-purpose flour)
  • ½ tsp black pepper + ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • ⅓–½ cup water (to make thick batter)
  • Oil, for deep/shallow frying

Instructions

  1. Make batter: Whisk cornflour, maida, pepper, salt, ginger-garlic and water to a thick, coating batter.
  2. Fry mushrooms: Pat dry mushrooms, toss in batter. Fry in hot oil (170–175°C) until crisp and light golden. Drain on a rack.
  3. Start sauce: Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok on high. Add garlic, ginger, chillies; stir-fry 20–30 sec until fragrant.
  4. Add veggies: Toss in onion petals, bell peppers and spring onion whites; stir-fry 1–2 min (keep them crunchy).
  5. Season: Add soy, chilli sauce, ketchup, vinegar, pepper and sugar. Mix well.
  6. Adjust consistency: Add water (¼ cup for dry; 1 cup for gravy). Bring to a boil.
  7. Thicken: Stir in cornflour slurry gradually until the sauce turns glossy and coats a spoon.
  8. Toss: Add fried mushrooms, toss just to coat. Check salt.
  9. Finish: Garnish with spring onion greens and sesame. Serve immediately.
