Chicken Biriyani

⬅ Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 500g Chicken (cleaned & cut)
  • 2 large Onions (sliced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cup Curd (yogurt)
  • 2 tbsp Biryani Masala
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Whole spices (cardamom, cinnamon, cloves)
  • Fresh Coriander & Mint leaves
  • Salt & Oil as needed

Instructions

  1. Soak basmati rice for 30 mins and parboil it.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and fry onions till golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, curd, and spices.
  4. Add chicken and cook until tender.
  5. Layer chicken masala and rice in a pot.
  6. Top with mint, coriander, and fried onions.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 mins (dum).
  8. Serve hot with raita.
