Malai Prawn

⬅ Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 500 g prawns, cleaned & deveined
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric + 1/2 tsp Kashmiri red chilli powder (for marination)
  • 1/2 tsp salt & 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp ghee (or oil)
  • 1 bay leaf, 2 green cardamoms, 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger‑garlic paste
  • 1 small green chilli, slit (optional)
  • 1 medium tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 200 ml coconut milk
  • 3 tbsp fresh cream (malai)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander & a squeeze of lemon to finish

Instructions

1.Marinate prawns with turmeric, chilli powder, salt and pepper. Rest 10 minutes.

2.Heat ghee. Lightly sear prawns 30–40 seconds each side; remove and keep aside.

3.In the same pan, add bay leaf, cardamom and cinnamon. Sauté a few seconds until aromatic.

4.Add onion and cook until soft and pale golden. Stir in ginger‑garlic paste and green chilli; cook 1 minute.

5.Add tomato, turmeric and coriander powder. Cook until tomatoes break down and the fat separates.

6.Pour in coconut milk, bring to a gentle simmer. Add salt to taste.

7.Return prawns to the pan and cook 3–4 minutes until just done.

8.Lower heat; stir in the fresh cream (malai). Simmer 1 minute. Finish with coriander and lemon.
