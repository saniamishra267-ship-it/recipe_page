Mutton Keema Kabab

⬅ Back to Home

Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 500 g minced mutton (keema)
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 green chillies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp mint leaves (chopped)
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp roasted gram flour (for binding)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil / ghee for shallow frying or grilling
  • Skewers (if grilling)

Instructions

  • In a large bowl, mix minced mutton with onion, chillies, ginger-garlic paste, coriander, mint, spices, lemon juice, and gram flour.
  • Knead the mixture well with your hands for 5–6 minutes until smooth.
  • Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes to 1 hour (helps flavors combine and keeps kababs firm).
  • Divide into equal portions and shape into round patties or onto skewers for seekh kababs.
  • Heat a tawa/pan with little oil OR preheat a grill/tandoor.
  • Cook kababs on medium-high heat until golden brown and fully cooked (8–10 minutes), turning occasionally.
  • Brush lightly with ghee for extra flavor before serving.
  • Serve hot with mint chutney, onion rings, and lemon wedges.
