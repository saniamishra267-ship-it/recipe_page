Veg Biriyani

⬅ Back to Home

Ingredients

  • For the chicken: 500 g boneless chicken thighs or breast, thinly sliced
  • 3 tbsp plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika (or red chilli powder)
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 tsp garlic powder or 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  • Mix yogurt, lemon, oil, spices, garlic, salt & pepper in a bowl. Add chicken and coat well. Marinate 15–30 minutes (or up to 2 hours in fridge).
  • Heat a skillet or grill pan on medium‑high. Cook chicken in batches 3–4 minutes per side until charred at edges and cooked through. Remove and rest 3 minutes, then slice thin if needed.
  • Make the sauce: whisk mayo, crushed garlic, lemon and salt. Taste. (Optional: for lighter sauce, use yogurt or a 50/50 mayo‑yogurt mix.)
  • Warm the wraps briefly on a dry pan or oven to make pliable. Spread a generous layer of garlic sauce down the center.
  • Layer chicken, fresh veggies, pickles, and fries (if using). Spoon more sauce and a drizzle of chilli sauce/hot sauce.
  • Fold sides and roll tightly (tuck with foil if serving later). Serve hot with extra sauce on the side.
    • Tips:
    • Use thighs for juicier shawarma; breasts are leaner and cook faster.
    • To mimic spit-roasted texture, finish cooked chicken under a hot broiler for 1–2 minutes to get crispy edges.
    • Make garlic sauce in advance — flavors mellow and improve after 30 minutes.
    • For halal/veg variation, swap chicken with grilled paneer or soya chunks and same spices.
