Veg Biriyani

⬅ Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, cauliflower)
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1/2 cup curd (yogurt)
  • 2 tbsp oil / ghee
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp biryani masala
  • Fresh coriander & mint leaves
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Cook till 70% done and set aside.

2. Heat oil/ghee, fry whole spices and onions till golden brown.

3. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and cook till soft.

4. Add vegetables, curd, biryani masala, coriander & mint leaves. Cook for 5-7 minutes.

5. In a pot, layer half-cooked rice and vegetable gravy. Repeat layers.

6. Cover tightly and cook on low flame for 15-20 minutes (dum method).

7. Serve hot with raita or salad.
