Paneer Tikka

⬅ Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 250 g paneer (cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 1 medium onion, petals
  • 1 capsicum (red/green), diced
  • ½ cup thick curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Skewers for grilling

Instructions

1.In a bowl, whisk curd with ginger-garlic paste, spices, lemon juice, salt, and oil.

2. Add paneer cubes, onion, and capsicum. Mix well so everything is coated.

3.Cover and marinate for at least 1 hour (or overnight in the fridge).

4.Thread paneer, onion, and capsicum alternately on skewers

5.Grill on a hot tandoor/oven at 200°C for 12–15 minutes OR on a pan until slightly charred.

6.Sprinkle chaat masala and serve hot with mint chutney & lemon wedges.

Enjoy your smoky Paneer Tikka with green chutney!
