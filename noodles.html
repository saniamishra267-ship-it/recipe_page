Chicken Noodles

⬅ Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 200 g boneless chicken (thin strips)
  • 200 g noodles (boiled & drained)
  • 1 medium onion (sliced)
  • 1 cup cabbage (shredded)
  • 1 carrot (julienned)
  • ½ cup capsicum (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chilli sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil noodles as per packet instructions. Drain, toss in a few drops of oil, and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok. Stir-fry chicken strips on high heat until cooked and slightly golden.
  3. Add onion, carrot, cabbage, and capsicum. Stir-fry 2–3 minutes (keep crunchy).
  4. Mix in soy sauce, chilli sauce, ketchup, vinegar, salt, and pepper.
  5. Add cooked noodles and toss well on high flame until coated with sauce.
  6. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
