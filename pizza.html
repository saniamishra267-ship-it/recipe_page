Kulhad Pizza

⬅ Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 4 small kulhads (food‑safe clay cups)
  • 6–8 bread slices or mini pizza bases (cubed)
  • 1 cup pizza sauce (or mix ketchup 2 tbsp + chilli sauce 1 tsp + oregano)
  • 1 cup mozzarella, grated (plus extra for topping)
  • 1/3 cup processed/cheddar, grated (stretch + flavor)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 capsicum, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, deseeded & chopped
  • Sweet corn, olives, jalapeños – as you like
  • 1 tsp mixed herbs (oregano, basil)
  • 1/2 tsp chilli flakes (adjust to taste)
  • Butter or olive oil for brushing
  • Salt & black pepper

Instructions

  1. kulhads:Preheat oven or OTG to 180°C. If using raw clay cups, rinse and dry fully. Lightly brush inside with butter or oil.
  2. Bread layer: Toss bread cubes with a spoon of butter and a pinch of herbs; briefly toast in a pan (optional, for crunch).
  3. Layering (repeat 2–3 times): In each kulhad add: bread cubes → 1–2 tbsp sauce → mixed veggies → mozzarella + cheddar → pinch of herbs & chilli flakes.
  4. Top & bake: Finish with extra cheese. Place kulhads on a tray; bake 8–12 minutes until cheese melts and bubbles. (Air‑fryer: 170°C for ~7–9 minutes.)
  5. Serve: Rest 2 minutes (hot clay!). Garnish with more herbs/chilli flakes. Eat straight from the kulhad with a spoon.
Tips:
  • No oven? Use a covered pan/tawa on very low heat 8–10 minutes to melt cheese.
  • Add a maggi masala pinch for that street‑style zing.
  • For non‑veg: add cooked chicken tikka shreds between layers.
  • Grease kulhad well so cheese doesn’t stick; don’t overfill to avoid spills.
